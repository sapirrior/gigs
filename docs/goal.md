# Our Goal: Simplifying Information Access and Creation with `gig`

At the heart of the `gig` engine and the `gigs` documentation project lies a straightforward and earnest philosophy: to make accessing and understanding technical information in the terminal environment as easy and intuitive as possible, and equally, to **make creating such pages easy.**

## Rooted in Tradition, Focused on Simplicity

It is important to clarify that `gig` is not an attempt to invent a wholly new documentation paradigm. Instead, its structure, syntax, and organizational principles are **based on the established and well-understood conventions of traditional Unix manual pages.** `gig` aims to modernize and enhance this proven format, making it more interactive and visually engaging for the terminal while retaining its familiar, structured approach.

## Not a Replacement, but an Aid

We recognize the invaluable contributions and long-standing utility of tools like `man` and `info` pages. The development of `gig` is not driven by a desire to discredit existing systems, nor does it assert itself as a "better" or "superior" alternative. Our ambition is not to build the "best" documentation system, but rather to humbly contribute a tool that genuinely helps.

## Focused on Ease and Clarity

Our primary objective with `gig` and the `gigs` you create is to:

*   **Simplify Access:** Provide a readily available, interactive, and visually appealing way to consume documentation directly within the terminal.
*   **Ease Creation:** Offer a clear, intuitive syntax that **tries to make creating pages easy**, allowing authors to focus on content rather than complex markup.
*   **Enhance Readability:** Leverage structured syntax, intelligent layout, and thoughtful styling to present information in a clear and digestible format.
*   **Foster Understanding:** Offer a framework that encourages well-organized, concise, and user-centric documentation.

`gig` aims to be a helpful companion in your terminal workflow, designed to streamline your access to information and make the process of learning, referencing, and *authoring* more enjoyable.
